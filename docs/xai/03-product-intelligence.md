# Xtreme AI Systems (XAI) Product Intelligence Spec

## 1. Product Evaluation Matrix
The Product Intelligence engine analyzes and maintains a catalog of exactly 20 commercial products related to surface preparation, polishing systems, and high-margin hardware or software extensions. Each product is scored against 10 critical parameters:
1.  **Search Demand:** Volume of organic searches.
2.  **Commercial Intent:** Propensity of searchers to buy.
3.  **Buyer Engagement:** Interaction rates on product specs.
4.  **Market Growth:** Year-over-year expansion percentage.
5.  **Repeat Purchase:** Likelihood of recurring consumable orders.
6.  **Margin Potential:** Gross margin of the product category.
7.  **Low-Friction Fulfillment:** Delivery simplicity.
8.  **Scalable Acquisition:** Customer acquisition cost feasibility.
9.  **Geographic Reach:** Delivery region expansion viability.
10. **Foreseeable ROI:** Projected timeframe for initial payback.

## 3. Operations & Refresh Schedule
*   **Refresh Times:** Exactly 3 times daily: **6:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 6:00 PM Eastern Time**.
*   **Process:** The Product Intelligence Agent queries public SEO databases, XPS order history, and search engine trends to recompute rankings.

## 4. Evidence Integrity Rules
All data points in the product index must carry one of the following explicit evidence labels:
*   `VERIFIED`: Supported by direct invoice records or API integration data.
*   `ESTIMATED`: Calculated using verified baseline formulas.
*   `INFERRED`: Modeled based on secondary indicators.
*   `UNAVAILABLE`: Data not accessible at run.
*   `REQUIRES VALIDATION`: Outlier flagged for operator review.

**Strict Prohibition:** Under no circumstances shall the system or any agent invent private clickstream data, competitor revenue, or confidential ad budgets. If data is unknown, it must be marked `UNAVAILABLE`.
