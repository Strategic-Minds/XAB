## 2. Mission Statement
Xtreme AI Systems (XAI) is established to design, build, and operate a titanium-grade autonomous operating system capable of running high-margin, automated visual-and-interactive commercial products. Our primary directive is the creation of fully autonomous visual pipelines that execute with perfect operational reliability, visual fidelity, and absolute margin safety.

## 3. Core Operating Principles
Every agent, system component, and human operator associated with XAI must adhere to the following absolute operating principles:
1.  **Evidence over Claims:** No architectural assertion, code change, or operational success is accepted based on assertion. All actions must be verified by durable, signed, and cryptographic validation receipts or BrowserWorker test captures.
2.  **Fail Closed:** In the event of network error, tool unavailability, auth failures, or visual discrepancies, the system must immediately halt, mark state as `BLOCKED`, and fail closed. We never permit "degraded bypass" of release gates.
3.  **Production Gated:** No artifact may transition to the production environment without passing 100% of the titanium release checklist. This is non-negotiable.
4.  **Revenue Before Hiring:** The operating system focuses on direct monetization and cash flow. Capital expansion is driven by recurring revenue, not speculative hiring or external funding.
5.  **Tools Must Create Action:** Any tool deployed in the XAI ecosystem must directly produce verifiable, auditable progress. Descriptive-only telemetry without automated remediation is a failure.

## 4. First Commercial Product
*   **Product Name:** Xtreme Scraper
*   **Classification:** Autonomous visual lead generation and market intelligence platform.
*   **Objective:** To crawl, scrape, visually index, and catalog concrete polishing projects, service providers, equipment listings, and commercial opportunities across all global regions to feed the Xtreme Polishing Systems sales pipeline.

## 5. Architectural Standards
*   **Canonical Browser Layer:** All visual verification and browser interactions are performed through the canonical endpoint: `https://browserworker.vercel.app`.
*   **Control Plane Authority:** The central governance and control authority resides exclusively in the `Strategic-Minds/XAB` repository under the main branch. No local state or branch overrides this source.

## 6. Economic Mandate
*   **Recurring Revenue:** All products generated must offer subscription or recurring transactional monetization paths.
*   **Return on Investment (ROI):** Every development initiative must have a quantified, evidence-based ROI calculation showing payback within 30 days.
*   **Qualified Opportunities:** Lead generation pipelines must deliver fully verified contact workbooks with high confidence ratings.
*   **Operational Growth:** Expansion is funded strictly through gross margins of active products.

## 7. Release Quality Standard
*   **100% Operational Success:** Zero console errors, zero network errors, and complete form/button execution on three consecutive test runs.
*   **99% Visual Parity:** 99% or greater structural and aesthetic alignment with approved layout specifications (visual contract) as calculated by automated pixel-comparison models.
