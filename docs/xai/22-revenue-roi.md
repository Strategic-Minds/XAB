# Xtreme AI Systems (XAI) Revenue and ROI Gate

## 1. Architectural Financial Modeling
To ensure complete economic safety, every business opportunity scored in the system must contain a comprehensive financial profile detailing:

*   **Startup Cost:** Tooling, domain, and database preparation expenses.
*   **Monthly Operating Cost:** API keys, database compute, and hosting.
*   **Cost Per Lead:** Scraping expense divided by validated outputs.
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Average advertising and organic outreach spend.
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** Projected transaction billing sizes.
*   **Gross Margin:** Financial efficiency percentages.
*   **Net Margin:** Factoring in taxes and platform fees.
*   **Break-Even Point:** Units or revenue required to offset initialization expenses.
*   **Time to First Revenue:** Estimated days to process initial invoice.
*   **Time to ROI:** Number of days required to yield positive net return.
*   **Cash Runway:** Safe operating runway under active expenses.
*   **Upsell Potential:** Accessory options or consulting tiers.
*   **Churn Risk:** Projected client attrition rate.
*   **Failure Probability:** Evaluated risk of market failure.

## 2. Opportunity Decision Classifications
Opportunities are automatically assigned one of four financial classifications:

*   **GO NOW (ROI $\le 30$ Days):** Extremely strong commercial demand, low CAC, and low startup costs. Automatically promoted to high-priority active build queues.
*   **VALIDATE FIRST (ROI 31-90 Days):** Solid indicators, but requires localized validation tests. Sent to human approval queue for targeted test funding.
*   **HOLD (Insufficient Evidence):** Lacks verified search metrics or commercial intent. Retained in the analysis bucket for historical tracking.
*   **DO NOT FUND:** Negative margin projections, no viable lead extraction source, or high saturation. Immediately archived.
