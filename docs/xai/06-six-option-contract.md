# Xtreme AI Systems (XAI) Six-Option Contract

## 1. The Six Mandated Options
For every single commercial idea selected for development, the system must generate exactly six materially different, high-fidelity launch packages. There must be zero cosmetic-only duplicates.

1.  **Lowest-Capital Option:** Minimizes startup infrastructure, utilizing lightweight tools, free tiers, and direct-to-customer organic outreach.
2.  **Fastest-Revenue Option:** Prioritizes immediate sales close cycles. Focuses on existing warm buyer databases, cold outreach, and pre-sales.
3.  **Highest-Margin Option:** High-ticket, bespoke configurations. Targets elite commercial firms who value lifetime warranty and specialized concrete treatments.
4.  **Strongest Recurring-Revenue Option:** Structured as a retainer, consumable replenishment plan, or software-as-a-service subscription.
5.  **Category/Local Dominance Option:** Hyper-aggressive search and local-citation saturation to capture 80%+ local market share.
6.  **GPT Evidence-Based Dealer's Choice:** An unconstrained proposal driven by historical data patterns, emerging search spikes, and direct visual trends.

## 2. Option Schema Requirements
Every one of the six options must supply detailed plans for all 34 required fields below:
1.  **business_name:** Proposed trade name.
2.  **brand_concept:** Psychological positioning statement.
3.  **logo_direction:** Aesthetic guidelines for icon and text.
4.  **color_system:** Exact HEX values for Primary, Secondary, Background, Accent.
5.  **typography:** Primary headings and secondary body font pairings.
6.  **target_buyer:** Specific industry niche and buyer persona.
7.  **buyer_psychology:** Key purchase drivers (efficiency, luxury, compliance).
8.  **need_vs_want:** Explicit classification of whether the offering is a regulatory need or aspirational want.
9.  **primary_offer:** Core value proposition.
10. **secondary_offer:** High-margin upsell or accessory.
11. **pricing_model:** Exact dollar pricing tiers (e.g., flat, tiered, per-unit).
12. **funnel_type:** Visual steps of the conversion journey.
13. **website_structure:** Site-map hierarchy and layout plan.
14. **ctas:** Exact call-to-action strings for every viewport.
15. **trust_strategy:** Verifiable badges, warranty terms, and compliance evidence.
16. **acquisition_strategy:** Direct, organic, paid, or scrapers.
17. **automation_strategy:** Post-purchase webhook triggers and auto-fulfillment plan.
18. **startup_cost:** Hardware and tooling initialization costs.
19. **monthly_operating_cost:** SaaS, hosting, API, and consumable costs.
20. **cost_to_compete:** Estimated advertising spend to enter market.
21. **cost_to_win:** Budget required to secure market dominance.
22. **time_to_first_customer:** Time-frame in days.
23. **time_to_first_revenue:** Projected invoicing date.
24. **break_even_point:** Net units/dollars to achieve neutrality.
25. **gross_margin:** Projected percentages.
26. **net_margin:** Factoring in taxes, fees, and API costs.
27. **expected_roi:** Detailed 3-month yield projections.
28. **failure_probability:** Statistical likelihood of project failure.
29. **main_failure_modes:** Concrete list of potential collapse causes.
30. **exact_mitigation_plan:** Actionable recovery steps for each failure mode.
31. **human_requirements:** Weekly hours required from human operator.
32. **automation_potential:** Percentage of process handled autonomously.
33. **launch_sequence:** Day-by-day rollout roadmap.
34. **growth_sequence:** Post-launch scale and customer expansion schedule.
